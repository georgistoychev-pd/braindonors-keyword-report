Architecture notes & structural decisions
  • No URL restructuring needed. The flat /services/ + /industry/ pattern is industry-standard and matches 3 of the 5 specified competitors. Avoid any 301 redirects on existing URLs that already carry ranking signals.
  • /services/google-ads already ranks at position 4 for "b2b google ads agency" (300/mo, KD 4) — this is the highest-confidence quick win. On-page optimisation of the existing page title, H1, and meta description alone should push this to position 1–3 within 2–4 months.
  • /industry/saas-marketing-agency is the #1 priority new page to build. The SERP has multiple weak DR entries (DR 22, DR 37) holding positions 2 and 5 with minimal backlinks. This is the most accessible high-intent gap in the entire cluster set.
  • SaaS and Fintech should each have dedicated /industry/ subpages to match the target market priority stated in the brief. These serve as vertical-specific conversion paths for inbound pipeline.
  • Homepage should target "b2b digital marketing agency" (2,500/mo) as primary keyword — the most competitive but highest-value long term play. All new /services/ and /industry/ pages should internally link back to the homepage for this term.
  • Consider adding /industry/manufacturing-marketing-agency as a future low-competition cluster (KD ~7, elevationb2b ranks position 11 with minimal effort).
